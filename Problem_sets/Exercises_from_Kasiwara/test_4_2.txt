Definitions and Notation

\begin{notation}
Let $\mathcal{C}$ be a small category.
\begin{itemize}
    \item $\mathcal{C}^{\wedge}$ denotes the category of presheaves on $\mathcal{C}$ (i.e., functors $\mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}} \to \mathbf{Set}$).
    \item $\mathrm{h}_{\mathcal{C}}: \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{C}^{\wedge}$ denotes the Yoneda embedding.
\end{itemize}
\end{notation}

\begin{definition}[Yoneda Extension]
Let $F: \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ be a functor. $\widehat{F}: \mathcal{C}^{\wedge} \to \mathcal{D}^{\wedge}$ denotes the \textbf{Yoneda extension} of $F$. It is defined as the left Kan extension of $\mathrm{h}_{\mathcal{D}} \circ F$ along $\mathrm{h}_{\mathcal{C}}$.
\[
\widehat{F} := \mathrm{Lan}_{\mathrm{h}_{\mathcal{C}}}(\mathrm{h}_{\mathcal{D}} \circ F)
\]
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[Left Exact Functor]
A functor $F: \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ is called \textbf{left exact} if for every object $Y$ in $\mathcal{D}$, the category $\mathcal{C} \times_{\mathcal{D}} \mathcal{D}_{/Y}$ is a cofiltered category.
\end{definition}
